STEPS FROM HOUSE TO MARKET, WHILE BUYING, BACK TO HOUSE
1. Make a list of a recipe.
2. Leave on your house to go in public market.
3. Walk from your house to public market.
4. Go to the public market.
5. Find the list of your recipe in public market.
6. Canvas your recipe prices, and check the quality of the recipe list.
7. Buy 1 kilogram of galunggong (130 Pesos).
8. Buy the next recipe on your list, 1 kilogram of pork sigang (150 Pesos).
9. Buy 1 kilogram of chicken wings (70 Pesos).
10. Buy recipes like onions, kangkong, tomatoes, and seasonings (70 Pesos).
11. If you're done, get out of the market.
12. Go back to your house.
13. Organize your ingredients.
